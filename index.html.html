About the Chapter

TUAA Guwahati Chapter was formed in 2018 with the vision to connect Tezpur University alumni residing in the Guwahati region. We foster professional networking, social bonding, and community outreach.

  • Formation: 15 March 2018
  • Objectives: Connect, support, and celebrate TU alumni.
  • MoA: Registered under Societies Act, 1860.
