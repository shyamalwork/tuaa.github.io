About the Chapter

A community built on shared roots

How we came together

The TUAA Guwahati Chapter was formed by alumni of Tezpur University residing and working in and around Guwahati, who recognised the value of staying connected as a community long after graduation. As the largest city in the Northeast and home to a significant concentration of Tezpur University graduates, Guwahati was a natural base for a dedicated chapter of the parent association.

The Chapter operates under a formally adopted Memorandum of Association (MoA), which lays out its objectives, governance structure, and the framework for elections and activities. This document guides every decision the Chapter makes and is available for members to review in our Documents section.

Why We Exist

Vision & Mission

The values that have guided the Chapter since its formation, rooted in the objectives set out in our Memorandum of Association.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, well-connected community of Tezpur University alumni in Guwahati that actively contributes to the growth of its members and the lasting legacy of the university.

Our Mission

  • Strengthen ties among alumni through regular meets and networking events
  • Support current students through mentorship and outreach initiatives
  • Celebrate and showcase the achievements of our alumni community
  • Maintain transparent governance as defined in our MoA
